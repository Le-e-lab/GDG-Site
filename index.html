Who We Are

GDG Africa University

We are a pan-African tech community at Africa University, equipping students with Google technologies and the skills to build Africa's digital future. Through peer-driven learning and hands-on projects, we bridge classroom theory with real-world impact.

The AU Influence

Our Core Pillars

Our mission is deeply rooted in Africa University's vision of a hunger-free, poverty-free, and peaceful continent. We align our technical training with this purpose, ensuring every line of code we write contributes to identifying and solving the most pressing challenges facing our communities.

Leadership Transformation

Developing tech leads who think "Africa-first". We cultivate leaders who can bridge technical excellence with social impact.

Study Jams • Tech Talks • Mentorship

Ubuntu in Tech

Peer-to-peer learning and community-driven coding. "I am because we are" guides how we build, learn, and grow together.

Pair Programming • Code Reviews • Open Source

Pan-African Innovation

Building solutions for local and continental challenges. From fintech to agritech, we solve real African problems.

Hackathons • Solution Challenges • Startups
